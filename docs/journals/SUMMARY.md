## Top picks (highest direct actionability)

- **`30_1909`** — SDF tendinopathy risk factors, Japan: ORs from pure race-DB fields (finishing pos ≥10th 2.50, sloppy track 1.69, distance <1300 m 1.45, fav rank ≥8 1.49, BW ≥470 kg 1.55, BW loss ≥5 kg 1.59, gelding 3.09, days-off ≥90 1.75). Injury-risk **and** performance signal.
- **`27_1514`** — JRA fracture epidemiology: track surface (dirt/turf), going grade, racecourse identity, corner geometry & distance affect both fracture rate **and racing time** (causal racetrack-reconstruction evidence).
- **`26_1506`** + **`21_4_73`** — Age→racing-speed curves. Speed peaks ~**4.0–4.5 yr** (monthly-resolution JRA; econometric US). Direct priors for age (quadratic/plateau) × distance × surface × sex.
- **`29_1802`** — Mixed-effects race-speed model: variance = horse 0.305 ≫ track 0.157 > sire 0.083 > trainer 0.064 > jockey 0.022. Validates horse-level effects ≫ pedigree; keeps weight-carried², age², temp, wind, going.
- **`28_1726`** — GWAS body weight in JRA Thoroughbreds → MSTN (chr18), LCORL (chr3); heritability 0.27–0.40. (`27_1611` adds LCORL→withers height/cannon circumference.)
- **`30_1901`** — Post-race EHI × WBGT at all 10 JRA racecourses (975k starts) → race-day climate features.
- **`35_2407` + `21_2_11` + `21_3_39`** — Pedigree/inbreeding: per-horse sire blood proportions (Sunday Silence, Northern Dancer, Deep Impact…) & inbreeding coefficients computable from JBBA records.
- **`18_2_47`** — Gait biomechanics: **overlap time** (limb-support overlap) correlates with race speed at r=−0.88 across a JRA G1 field → motivates sectional-time / late-race-speed-drop features.
- **`13_4_117` + `14_3_93` + `18_4_153`** — Aerobic-fitness metrics for JRA horses: **V200** (speed at HR 200), submaximal canter HR (separates open-class & male horses), in-race HRpeak ~214 bpm / post-race lactate ~22.5 mmol/L.
- **`13_2_41`** — JRA SDFT tendonitis review: track surface×going interaction, career distance/days, layup history as injury/wastage features (70% don't return).
- **`12_1_1`** — **Ban-ei**: body size/conformation (PC1) predicts earnings — relevant to the Ban-ei race category.
- **`5_2_53`** — JRA best-racing-times model: quantified fixed effects (sex, age, surface, going, track) + **surface×going sign-flip** (poor _dirt_ → faster, poor _turf_ → slower) + cross-distance sire r=0.35 → separate sprint/middle aptitude. The single most pipeline-aligned modelling paper.
- **`5_4_127`** — Start-dash mechanics: early acceleration is stride-frequency-limited, peak speed stride-length-limited → gate-break quality / first-furlong time as biomechanically-grounded features.
- **PCV/Hb as a performance feature** (`10_3,4_61` `10_3,4_67` `9_4_107` `11_3_51` `6_2_61`): splenic erythrocyte release drives the exercise PCV surge; splenectomy cuts peak speed ~14%. Pre-race haematocrit/Hb is mechanistically performance-gating.
