## Top picks (highest direct actionability)

- **`30_1909`** — SDF tendinopathy risk factors, Japan: ORs from pure race-DB fields (finishing pos ≥10th 2.50, sloppy track 1.69, distance <1300 m 1.45, fav rank ≥8 1.49, BW ≥470 kg 1.55, BW loss ≥5 kg 1.59, gelding 3.09, days-off ≥90 1.75). Injury-risk **and** performance signal.
- **`27_1514`** — JRA fracture epidemiology: track surface (dirt/turf), going grade, racecourse identity, corner geometry & distance affect both fracture rate **and racing time** (causal racetrack-reconstruction evidence).
- **`26_1506`** + **`21_4_73`** — Age→racing-speed curves. Speed peaks ~**4.0–4.5 yr** (monthly-resolution JRA; econometric US). Direct priors for age (quadratic/plateau) × distance × surface × sex.
- **`29_1802`** — Mixed-effects race-speed model: variance = horse 0.305 ≫ track 0.157 > sire 0.083 > trainer 0.064 > jockey 0.022. Validates horse-level effects ≫ pedigree; keeps weight-carried², age², temp, wind, going.
- **`28_1726`** — GWAS body weight in JRA Thoroughbreds → MSTN (chr18), LCORL (chr3); heritability 0.27–0.40. (`27_1611` adds LCORL→withers height/cannon circumference.)
- **`30_1901`** — Post-race EHI × WBGT at all 10 JRA racecourses (975k starts) → race-day climate features.
- **`35_2407` + `21_2_11` + `21_3_39`** — Pedigree/inbreeding: per-horse sire blood proportions (Sunday Silence, Northern Dancer, Deep Impact…) & inbreeding coefficients computable from JBBA records.
- **`18_2_47`** — Gait biomechanics: **overlap time** (limb-support overlap) correlates with race speed at r=−0.88 across a JRA G1 field → motivates sectional-time / late-race-speed-drop features.
- **`13_4_117` + `14_3_93` + `18_4_153`** — Aerobic-fitness metrics for JRA horses: **V200** (speed at HR 200), submaximal canter HR (separates open-class & male horses), in-race HRpeak ~214 bpm / post-race lactate ~22.5 mmol/L.
- **`13_2_41`** — JRA SDFT tendonitis review: track surface×going interaction, career distance/days, layup history as injury/wastage features (70% don't return).
- **`12_1_1`** — **Ban-ei**: body size/conformation (PC1) predicts earnings — relevant to the Ban-ei race category.
- **`5_2_53`** — JRA best-racing-times model: quantified fixed effects (sex, age, surface, going, track) + **surface×going sign-flip** (poor _dirt_ → faster, poor _turf_ → slower) + cross-distance sire r=0.35 → separate sprint/middle aptitude. The single most pipeline-aligned modelling paper.
- **`5_4_127`** — Start-dash mechanics: early acceleration is stride-frequency-limited, peak speed stride-length-limited → gate-break quality / first-furlong time as biomechanically-grounded features.
- **PCV/Hb as a performance feature** (`10_3,4_61` `10_3,4_67` `9_4_107` `11_3_51` `6_2_61`): splenic erythrocyte release drives the exercise PCV surge; splenectomy cuts peak speed ~14%. Pre-race haematocrit/Hb is mechanistically performance-gating.

## Top picks (馬の科学)

- **[vol55-no3 p.189](horse-sciences/vol55-no3-2018-09.md)** (G) — Age → mean race-speed curve on JRA flat (2002–2010): speed rises to ~3yr-Mar–early-4yr then plateaus; carried-weight peaks 4yr-Jun–Aug. The single most pipeline-aligned prior for **age × month × distance × sex × 斤量** speed correction.
- **[vol56-no3 p.194](horse-sciences/vol56-no3-2019-09.md)** (F) — Seasonal body-weight model from 632,540 JRA horses: colts/geldings peak autumn-winter / trough summer, fillies peak autumn / trough spring, ~30 kg gain through age 5. Direct **seasonal × sex × growth** 馬体重 baseline correction.
- **[vol53-no2 p.92](horse-sciences/vol53-no2-2016-06.md)** (A) — 700k-start fracture epidemiology: McⅢ fractures = dirt/winter-spring/male-gelding/3yo turf-long; P1 = maiden/Tokyo-Kyoto. A ready **sex×age×distance×surface×season** fracture-risk baseline from pure race-DB fields.
- **[vol51-no2 p.132](horse-sciences/vol51-no2-2014-06.md)** (A) — 239,579-horse carpal-fracture logistic model: dirt 1.012% > turf 0.704%, risk ↑ for short distance / heavy BW / ≤4yo / Miho. Odds ratios on fields the pipeline already has.
- **[vol54-no2 p.116](horse-sciences/vol54-no2-2017-06.md)** (A) — McⅢ fracture × training content: ≥6 fast works/30d OR 0.515, start count OR 0.430, Polytrack OR 2.167 — work-load and training-surface as injury signals.
- **[vol52-no3 p.235](horse-sciences/vol52-no3-2015-09.md)** + **[p.237](horse-sciences/vol52-no3-2015-09.md)** (A) — UK race-related sudden-death case-control (older / long-distance / summer / few starts in last 60d) and a bone-fatigue model (age/sex/track-hardness/speed/distance/weight). **Race-spacing & distance** wastage priors.
- **[vol51-no2 p.122](horse-sciences/vol51-no2-2014-06.md)** + **[p.124](horse-sciences/vol51-no2-2014-06.md)** (D) — MSTN polymorphism → V200/distance aptitude, and LCORL `BIEC2-808543` → withers height / body weight. Core **distance-aptitude + body-size** genotype markers (see MSTN cross-ref above).
- **[vol56-no2 p.110](horse-sciences/vol56-no2-2019-06.md)** + **[vol52-no3 p.240](horse-sciences/vol52-no3-2015-09.md)** + **[vol51-no4 p.348](horse-sciences/vol51-no4-2014-12.md)** (E/A/F) — Track-surface/going effects: Polytrack cushion-hardness 80.5→58.5G + drainage ×1.6–2.5 (time signal); synthetic vs dirt stress-fracture detection 31.7% vs 23.0%; turf vs all-weather hoof-ground loading differences. **Going/surface** correction & injury risk.
- **[vol54-no2 p.90](horse-sciences/vol54-no2-2017-06.md)** + **[p.94](horse-sciences/vol54-no2-2017-06.md)** (C) — Late-rearing **V200** linked to race results/earnings in fillies; hypoxic training raises VO₂max +12.1% with gluteal capillarisation. Aerobic-fitness proxies and a filly-specific prognostic signal.
- **[vol50-no2 p.116](horse-sciences/vol50-no2-2013-06.md)** (G, title-only) + **[vol56-no4 p.359](horse-sciences/vol56-no4-2019-12.md)** (G) — Per-racecourse **accident-rate baselines** and a jockey-fall/equine-fatal-injury epidemiology (29–44% of falls linked to fatal breakdown). Course-level scratch/DNF priors.
- **[vol51-no1 p.80](horse-sciences/vol51-no1-2014-03.md)** + **[vol51-no2 p.100](horse-sciences/vol51-no2-2014-06.md)** + **[vol56-no4 p.368](horse-sciences/vol56-no4-2019-12.md)** (B) — Laryngeal/airway poor-performance: palate-instability/ADAF stratifies DDSP, ~80% of active horses show exercise-only upper-airway obstruction missed at rest, yearling dynamic endoscopy as a future-performance flag. **Respiratory under-performance** features.
- **[vol55-no4 p.269](horse-sciences/vol55-no4-2018-12.md)** + **[vol56-no2 p.92](horse-sciences/vol56-no2-2019-06.md)** (A) — Return-to-racing after SDFT tendinopathy (only 14.2% make ≥5 starts; longitudinal lesion length drives difficulty) and after condylar fracture (surgery 67–86% vs conservative 40–44%, ~260–318d layup). **Injury-history → return-probability** baselines.
- **[vol56-no4 p.360](horse-sciences/vol56-no4-2019-12.md)** + **[p.374](horse-sciences/vol56-no4-2019-12.md)** (F) — Wireless multi-IMU gait systems quantifying trunk/limb asymmetry to ±2–5 mm, separating fore- vs (compensatory) hind-limb lameness by sign/timing. Foundation for **gait-asymmetry** features.
